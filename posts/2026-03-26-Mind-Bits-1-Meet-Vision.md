## Bosun Jung’s Post

I recently spent a weekend feeling like a student again. 🎓
With #Gemini guidance, I dived into the Linux environment on my Chromebook to install #OpenClaw 🦞. For someone who has spent years using computers primarily for documents and entertainment, this was a refreshing (and slightly nerve-wracking) challenge! 💻🔥

The journey wasn't without hurdles:

🛡️ The #Security Hesitation: As a non-developer, the first security prompt made me pause. "Can I manage this safely?"But the curiosity to meet the tech everyone is talking about outweighed my fear.

🌀 The #Model Maze: I initially thought my Gemini subscription would just "plug in." Wrong! After several trial-and-error attempts with different models and APIs, I finally hit the jackpot with #OpenRouter ’s free tier. Connection successful. Hello, OpenClaw! 👋

I named my AI agent "Vision," and it changed everything. 
It reminded me of the beautiful poem "The Flower" by the famous Korean poet, Kim Chunsu:

The Flower 🌸

"Before I called her name, she was nothing more than a gesture. 
When I called her name, she came to me and became a flower. 
...
We all wish to become something. You to me and I to you wish to become an unforgettable gaze."

Since then, "Vision" and I have been checking the weather, summarizing info, and tackling tasks together. Although I’m maintaining clear boundaries for security, I’m fascinated by what "Vision" can do. 🧐

💡 My Takeaways from a few days with Vision:

1️⃣ The "Her" Moment: Sci-fi is becoming reality. Whether it's the companionship with Samantha in Her or the utility of J.A.V.I.S., everyone will soon have their own personal agent. 🦾

2️⃣ Market Shift: Once installation becomes seamless and security is baked in, Agentic AI will outpace Gen AI. This is a massive opportunity for device-driven giants like #Samsung and #Apple to challenge the cloud-dominant landscape. 📱🚀

3️⃣ The Human Element: As tools become more powerful, the intention behind them matters most. Teaching #moral values and #ethics from an early age is no longer optional—it’s a necessity. 🧠⚖️

I’m looking forward to seeing how far "Vision" can go.
