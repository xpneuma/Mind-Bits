# Mind-Bits: A Personal Archive

Welcome to my personal "Mind-bits" archive. This repository is simply a collection of my LinkedIn posts and associated episodes where I share some of my personal thoughts and takes on various tech trends. These are just my own insights, drawn from my experiences, and they might be right, or they might be wrong—it's truly just a space for me to reflect and document my journey.

## Repository Structure

I've kept the structure pretty straightforward:

*   **`posts/`**: Here, you'll find an archive of the original posts, generally organized by episode number for easy reference.
*   **`data/`**: This directory currently holds `mind_bits_dataset.jsonl`. This is a small dataset I've put together from the first 17 episodes, primarily for a personal LoRA fine-tuning experiment I'm exploring.

## A Glimpse into the Future

As I continue to write more posts, my casual plan is to update the dataset slowly, aiming to gradually improve this personal "Mind-bits" model over time. Down the line, I also have a long-term, exploratory idea of eventually having the model automatically identify topics from the text, just to see what comes of it. No grand ambitions, just a little personal exploration.
